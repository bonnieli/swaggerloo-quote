FAQ

Does this quoting form calculate the actual total cost?

Although we try to be as accurate as possible with this online quoting form, some prices may differ depending on what customers are actually looking for. When we look over your order, we will confirm every detail just to make sure what you have quoted matches exactly what you are looking for! The final price will be calculated once details have been communicated throughly to our Swaggerloo representatives and confirmed. Contact Us!

I'm ready to order! What's my next step?

Awesome! Head on over to our contact page, copy and paste the table of information so we know exactly what you're looking for and a Swaggerloo representative will contact you as soon as possible!

Does Swaggerloo do embroidery?

Yes! At this moment, we do not have an online quoting form for embroidery but feel free to contact us for more details!

What is the minimum and maximum quantity order?

Minimum is 1, and maximum is 999. However, Swaggerloo recommends customers to purchase a minimum quantity of 12. The quoting form will give you a good idea of why we recommend a quantity of 12+.

I am looking for something specific.

If you don't think the quoting form is helping you, feel free to contact us!

What is personalization?

Add a little personalization to every apparel! This is a popular choice for student groups and residence buildings. Maximum of two words (perhaps first and last name) per personalization.
